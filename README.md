## About Me

I am a Full Stack and Backend Engineer and a graduate student at **Northeastern University, Boston**, pursuing a **Master of Science in Information Systems** (graduating May 2026, GPA: 3.85).

Most recently I completed a **Software Engineer Co-op at Teradyne**, where I worked on event-driven backend systems, hardware diagnostics workflows, and production reliability on a real engineering team. Before that I spent two years as a **Software Engineer at IDFC First Bank**, building high-throughput Java and Python services handling millions of financial transactions daily.

My interests sit at the intersection of **backend engineering, full stack development, and applied AI**. I have been building with LLM APIs, RAG pipelines, and tools like LangChain and LlamaIndex, and I enjoy shipping things that actually work in production — not just in notebooks.

I believe in writing code that others can understand, systems that fail gracefully, and always asking why before asking how.
